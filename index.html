About Gloss and Garb

Our Story (From the CEO herself)

Hi gorgeous, I'm Khadijah, Khaddie for short. I'm a penultimate student at the University of Ibadan and a soon-to-be handmade lip care products and beauty essentials entrepreneur. I'll be sharing the story behind my brand name "Gloss and Garb" and what drives my passion for handmade lip care products and beauty essentials. As someone that has been a sucker for lipglosses for a very long time, I've always dreamed of creating my own products. Despite initial fears and doubts about entering a saturated market, I'm determined to turn my passion into a successful business. I really can't remember how I came up with the name "Gloss and Garb", but I really love it. Then I checked online and saw that there was no other brand with the name, and that made everything perfect for me. I know "Garb" refers to attire, but in this context, it refers to accessories or anything at all that enhances your appearance. I would love for you to join me on this journey. Stay tuned for more updates 💕
